Unleash your startup's true potential with expert guidance and tailored solutions that drive growth, professionalization, and sustainable success.

Let's talk

Navigating the startup landscape is a demanding journey, often accompanied by unforeseen hurdles. As your business expands rapidly, inefficiencies may arise, hindering your progress. Empower Startups is here to alleviate these burdens, providing seasoned executive support that aligns with your growth aspirations.

Expertise services

Strategy

  • Strategy Review and Development: Refine your overall strategy and business model for sustainable growth.
  • Deck Review and Development: Enhance your investor deck to effectively communicate your value proposition and secure funding.
  • Value Proposition Review and Redefinition: Strengthen your value proposition to clearly convey the unique benefits of your products or services.

Business Planning

  • Comprehensive Business Plan Creation: Develop a detailed business plan with financial projections, risk assessments, and strategic growth plans.
  • Financial Analysis and Risk Management: Conduct thorough financial audits, identify and mitigate potential risks, and optimize financial performance.
  • Dynamic Financial Modeling: Create an adaptable financial model incorporating key assumptions, variables, KPIs, and scenario planning.

Financial & Reporting

  • Financial Audits and Reporting: Perform comprehensive financial audits and generate accurate financial reports for informed decision-making.
  • Funding Strategy Development: Identify and secure appropriate funding sources, including investors, banks, and other financing options.
  • Investor Documentation Preparation: Prepare investor-ready documents to effectively present your business and attract potential investors.

About me - Ferran Vilà

With a unique blend of entrepreneurial drive, consulting expertise, and a passion for supporting others, I bring a multifaceted approach to Empower Startups. As the founder and CEO of my own startup, I have firsthand experience in navigating the challenges and opportunities of the startup landscape. This journey has instilled in me a deep understanding of what it takes to transform an idea into a thriving business. My passion for empowering aspiring entrepreneurs led me to mentoring early-stage startups. Through this mentorship, I have had the privilege of sharing my insights, experiences, and network, helping them overcome obstacles, refine their strategies, and secure the resources they need to succeed.

Prior to immersing myself in the startup world, I honed my analytical and creative skills as a business consultant for large corporations. This experience provided me with a solid foundation in business fundamentals, market analysis, and strategic planning. I learned to balance analytical rigor with creative problem-solving, an approach that has proven invaluable in helping startups achieve sustainable growth.

At Empower Startups, I combine my entrepreneurial passion, consulting expertise, and mentorship experience to provide comprehensive support for startups. I am committed to guiding startups through every stage of their journey, from strategy development and funding acquisition to operational optimization and investor relations. My goal is to empower startups to achieve their full potential and make a lasting impact.
